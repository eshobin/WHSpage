Walpole High School was founded in 1870. It offers
many clubs, sports and extracurricular activities for students
to pursure their interests.Walpole High School is ranked
141st within Massachusetts. With such expectations and
support, over 90% of its graduates typically pursue higher
education, competing successfully for admission to the most
selective and prestigious colleges and universities in the nation.
WHS is accredited by the New England Association
of Secondary Schools.
